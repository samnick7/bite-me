FAQ

We do not have a car park but we do have an agreement with La Concha car park, which is a 10-minute walk away along La Concha Promenade.

We do not have a set kids’ menu but we do offer alternatives for children on request.

You can book a table on the terrace. However, if the weather is bad, these reservations are not guaranteed because we cannot be sure that the reservation can be moved to the dining room (it may be full).

If the weather is bad, reservations on the terrace are cancelled as there are no awnings or glass enclosures to allow us to serve food.

In any event, we always try to move all reservations inside the restaurant.

You can ask for a table with a view and your request will be noted. However, we cannot guarantee it as the front-of-house manager decides the layout of the tables each day, based on the number of reservations.

You can add a meal, lunch or dinner if you want. We have a Seasonal Menu and Bite Me Restaurant Tasting Menu, with drinks included.
